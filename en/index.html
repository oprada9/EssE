History

EssE began in April 2020 as a solo project of the band's guitarist, Oscar Prada, in the city of Cartagena de Indias, Colombia. In May of that same year, John Sierra decided to enter the project after listening to the first mock-up, which months later would be called "Letanías al Verdugo".

After rehearsing with several vocalists during the songwriting process, they received a call from Quique who told them that he was interested in joining EssE as a bassist and vocalist, so they did not hesitate to accept his proposal. In this way, the lineup that would materialize his first EP entitled "Letanías al Verdugo" was completed, which will be released in the coming months.

The name EssE refers to the verb To Be in Latin, which in a less literal sense alludes to the existence of being. The theme of the band mainly covers the Colombian reality, generating a syncretism between politics, violence and religion, and how these factors permeate the corrupt and criminal culture that unfortunately are part of the daily life and the idiosyncrasy of many people in this -our- territory.
